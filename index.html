Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

GamerHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Data Collection & Usage

We believe in privacy by design. GamerHub does NOT collect, store, sell, or transmit your personal data to any external servers owned by the developer. All data processing occurs locally on your device or directly between your browser and the official third-party APIs.

2. Permissions

  • Storage: Used solely to save your local preferences (e.g., watchlist, API keys) on your own browser.
  • Cookies: Used to securely access your existing login session on the Steam Community website to fetch your friends list without requiring a separate login.
  • Alarms: Used to schedule periodic background checks for notifications.
  • Host Permissions: Required to communicate directly with game service providers (Steam, Faceit, CheapShark).

3. Third-Party Services

GamerHub acts as a dashboard that aggregates data from official services (Steam, Faceit, CheapShark). We do not have access to your login credentials.
