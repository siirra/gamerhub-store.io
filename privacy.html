01. Core Philosophy (Local Processing)

GamerHub is engineered with Privacy by Design. We operate as a client-side tool, meaning we do not own, operate, or rent servers to store your personal gaming data.

All data fetched (Friends, Prices, Stats) is processed and stored locally on your machine using chrome.storage.local. Your data never leaves your device to reach us.

02. Permissions Usage
  • Cookies: Used solely to access your active steamcommunity.com session. This enables friend list syncing without re-login. We NEVER access your password.
  • Storage: Used to save your settings, watchlist, and API keys locally.
  • Host Permissions: Required to communicate with Steam, Faceit, and CheapShark APIs directly from your browser.

03. Third-Party Connections

The extension communicates directly with the following endpoints:

  • Steam APIs: Profile & Friend Data.
  • Faceit API: Player Stats & Elo.
  • CheapShark/GamerPower: Prices & Giveaways.
  • Cloudflare/AWS: Network Speed Tests.

04. Monetization & Affiliates

GamerHub is free. However, to support development, some links in the "Deals" tab may be affiliate links (e.g., Instant Gaming, G2A). If you purchase a game through these links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

05. Data Control & Removal

Since we do not store your data on our servers, you have full control:

  • To Delete Data: Simply uninstall the extension from Chrome. This instantly wipes all locally stored keys and settings.
  • To Reset: You can use the "Delete Key" buttons in the extension settings to remove API keys individually.
