Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 01, 2026

GamerHub ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains clearly how our Chrome Extension interacts with your data. We believe in transparency and privacy by design.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit your personal data to any external servers owned by GamerHub.

All data processing happens locally on your machine or directly between your browser and the official game APIs (Steam, Faceit, CheapShark). We have no database of our users.

2. Permissions Usage

We request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function:

  • Storage: Used to save your settings (like API keys, watchlist, and theme preferences) locally in your browser.
  • Cookies: Used to access your existing active session on steamcommunity.com. This allows the extension to fetch your friends list securely without asking for your login credentials.
  • Alarms: Used to schedule background checks (e.g., checking for new game deals or friend activity) without keeping the popup open.
  • Host Permissions: Required to fetch data from external gaming services (Steam API, Faceit API, CheapShark).

3. Third-Party Services

GamerHub acts as a client-side dashboard. It communicates directly with the following third-party services on your behalf:

  • Valve Corporation (Steam): To retrieve friend status and profile info.
  • Faceit: To retrieve player Elo and match stats.
  • CheapShark & GamerPower: To retrieve game prices and free giveaways.

We are not affiliated with Valve or Faceit. Your interaction with these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.
