Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 01, 2026

GamerHub ("we", "our", or "us") provides this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Data Collection & Security (Local Processing)

We are committed to Privacy by Design. GamerHub operates as a client-side tool.

  • No Remote Storage: We do not own, operate, or rent any servers to store your personal data. All data fetched (Friends list, Prices, Stats) is processed and stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local.
  • API Keys: If you provide your Steam or Faceit API keys, they are stored unencrypted but locally within your browser's secure storage area. They are never transmitted to us.

2. Detailed Permissions Usage

To function correctly, GamerHub requires specific permissions. Here is how we use each one:

A. Cookies (Critical Functionality)

We use the cookies permission to access your active session on steamcommunity.com. This allows the extension to:

  • Retrieve your friends list and their online status without requiring you to sign in again inside the extension.
  • We do NOT access, store, or transmit your password or login credentials.

B. Host Permissions (External Connections)

The extension communicates directly with the following third-party services:

  • *.steampowered.com & *.steamcommunity.com: To fetch user profiles and friend activity.
  • *.faceit.com: To fetch player statistics and Elo ratings (requires API Key).
  • *.cheapshark.com & *.gamerpower.com: To fetch real-time game prices and free giveaways.
  • *.cloudflare.com: Used solely for the "Network Speed Test" feature to measure download/upload latency.

3. Affiliate Disclosure & Monetization

Transparency is key. GamerHub is a free-to-use extension, but we may earn revenue in the following ways:

Affiliate Links: Some links to game stores (e.g., Instant Gaming, G2A, CDKeys) within the "Deals" section may be affiliate links. This means if you click on a link and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps support the development of the extension.

4. Network & Speed Test

The "Network" tab performs a real-time latency test (Ping) and bandwidth speed test. By using this feature, you acknowledge that the extension will send dummy data packets to Cloudflare and AWS DynamoDB endpoints to measure connection quality. No personal identifier is associated with these tests.

5. User Rights & Data Removal

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can clear all stored data (API Keys, Watchlist, Settings) by simply removing the extension from Chrome.
  • You can manually clear cached data via the extension's "Settings" panel (e.g., "Delete Key").
